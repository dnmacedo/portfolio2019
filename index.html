about me

I attended the University of Califoria Davis where I studied my two passions design (specializing in graphics) and psychology. After graduating, I worked for a non-profit as a Junior Graphic Designer, at the Veterans Association of Real Estate Professionals. I then went on to work as an Account Coordinator/Designer for a marketing agency called Mindshare Creative.

My main passions are art and psychology, and my core expertise is in graphic design, but I am also increasingly getting interested in UX and UI. Having a solid front end development foundation and a basic understanding of html, css and javascript (what this site is built on), I am currently improving my skills for web design and development through my online curriculum at Skillcrush.com.
