The Flow
2016 | app concept + video

In a specialized design class focused on water conservation, the final project was to develop an interactive (physical or digital) experience to promote awareness or participation in helping the water crisis in California, and create a promotional video for it using After Effects.

We created a concept for an app where people could educate themselves about how much water gets wasted through common water leaks, and then empowers and equips them to report water leaks in their own city.
