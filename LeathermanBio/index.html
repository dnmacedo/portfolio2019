Leatherman BioConsulting, Inc.
2017 | brand identity design

When working with Leatherman BioConsulting, Inc. I got to work with two incredibly smart biologists who run a small business in my home city, Yorba Linda. To quote from their website: "Leatherman BioConsulting, Inc. specializes in terrestrial biological resource surveys, management, and monitoring. We offer an array of biological services including threatened and endangered species surveys, nesting bird surveys and management, habitat restoration, construction compliance monitoring, exotic species control, wildlife corridor use analysis, and data collection for conservation planning efforts."

They were seeking to rebrand their company and put me up to the task! Along with creating their new logo, I revised various other business materials such as their business cards, stationary, powerpoint, flyer and website (built on Wix). The idea behind the new branding identity was to illustrate the marriage of the company's expertise and related services in both plants and wildlife, which are also the individual strengths of the two founders, Mrs. and Mr. Leatherman respectively.

|


Myself and the Client both wanted the logo to speak true to their work and services as well as their background. Providing images of plants and animals they took in the field, I incorporated them into various designs; they settled on the above logo. I'm glad they were able to take part in the design concept process, because I feel it's important for a business owner to actully identify with their branding identity, so adding their personal touch made it feel like their own.
